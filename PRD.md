# Project: GTM Intelligence & Market Response Hub
**Status:** Prototype v1.0  

---

## 1. Problem Statement
Go-to-Market (GTM) teams in high-growth technology sectors operate in hyper-competitive landscapes where market shifts occur weekly. Fragmented technical news, competitor product launches, and shifting industry benchmarks create significant "noise." This information overload delays sales response times, results in missed opportunities, and leads to inconsistent market positioning across the organization.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Primary: Enterprise Sales & Account Teams**
    *   *Need:* Immediate, actionable "objection handling" and value-proposition pivots during active deal cycles.
*   **Secondary: GTM & Product Leadership**
    *   *Need:* High-level visibility into competitive density, market trends, and strategic threat assessments to inform long-term roadmap decisions.

## 3. Functional Requirements
*   **FR1: Automated Ingestion**
    *   Capability to ingest unstructured data from diverse sources (RSS feeds, news API, or manual entry) into a centralized relational database.
*   **FR2: AI-Driven Synthesis**
    *   Implementation of Large Language Models (LLMs) to transform raw telemetry into structured Sales Battlecards and situational talk tracks.
*   **FR3: Dynamic Risk Scoring**
    *   Automated categorization of competitor moves into "Threat Levels" (Low to Critical) based on market impact and product overlap.
*   **FR4: Executive Command Center**
    *   A centralized dashboard providing real-time visualization of competitive velocity and distribution of market threats for cross-functional stakeholders.
